This key (mentioned as VK_APPS in WinUser.h) located between RightWin and RightShift on most keyboard layouts.

Sometimes it's called Application/Menu. Thought, it is not correct in Windows because VK_MENU (from WinUser.h) refers to the Alt key in fact.
